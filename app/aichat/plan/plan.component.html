* Please log out and log in again. Check plans under prepaid, postpaid and dongle.

* Prepaid is a telecom service plan where you are billed a predetermined amount before you can use the services. Plans vary from 24 days to 1 year.

* Postpaid is a telecom service plan where you are billed a predetermined amount at the end of the month of usage. The month is called the billing cycle and may not refer to a calendar month.

* A dongle is a small USB 'internet stick' that allows you to access online services without needing to connect to Wi-Fi. Plans for dongle are mentioned on the home page.
